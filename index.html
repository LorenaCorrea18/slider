2 / 20

From the "Select filter" menu, select the Work Package option and press the enter key or go to the search icon to filter the required information.

3 / 20

You will find basic information on the requested Work Package number.

4 / 20

Click the blue "Site Services request" button located at the bottom left, as shown in the following image.

5 / 20

Once you select the "Site Services Request" option, display the options in the "ZI partner" field and choose one, according to the corresponding user data.

6 / 20

You will find two blue options, located to the right of the "ZI Partner" field selection box.

7 / 20

Option number one, will allow you to review the data of the "ZI Partner" previously selected, without making any modifications, as shown in the following image.

8 / 20

Option number two is use to add a new "ZI partner" according to the user's IPM data.

9 / 20

After completing the "Zi Partner" field, you must update the information in the "Services" format that is in the "Incomplete" state, by displaying the form by clicking on the "Services" gray bar.

10 / 20

Once the "Services" process is deploy proceed to fill in the form fields as follows. Select the options in the "Work item" field, if none of these is what you need, click on the blue icon located to the right side of the option, the system will automatically update new options.

11 / 20

When the "Work item" option is update correctly, it generates a validation box, as shown below.

12 / 20

Then you must add an option by displaying the "WBS" field, if the field is empty, click the button with the "+" icon located to the right of the option to generate the WBS information.

13 / 20

To generate new information "WBS" will open the window you will see below, display and select the options as appropriate, remember to click on the green button "Save changes" to save changes or the button red "Close "To exit the window.

14 / 20

Once the WBS information is select, add the "Cost package" information for that service by clicking on the gray "+" icon, as shown in the image below.

15 / 20

Then, deploy and select the "VOA / Supplier" information as appropriate, as shown below.

16 / 20

To add comments, click on the "Add new comments" field, as shown below.

17 / 20

Add the necessary comments in the "Comments" window that is automatically displayed, as shown in the image, click the green "Save changes" button to keep comments or the red "Close" button to close the window.

18 / 20

Make sure that the "Services" form is in the "Complete" state as indicated by the image.

19 / 20

After filling all the fields in the "Services" form, click on the green button "Save changes" to keep the changes made. You can save the changes in the "Incomplete" state and resume the modifications without losing the previous changes.

20 / 20

To finish click on the blue button "Send SSR" as indicated by the following image. You will receive an email confirming the status of your process.
